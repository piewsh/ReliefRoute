Why ReliefRoute?

ReliefRoute empowers emergency responders to find the fastest, safest paths in the wake of a disaster. Upload your delivery points, tune your fleet settings, and let our engine calculate precision-optimized routes in seconds.

1. Upload Your Data

Upload a CSV file or manually select destination points on the map.

2. Configure Your Fleet

Set vehicle counts, capacities, time windows and service durations.

3. Get Optimized Routes

Our solver returns the most efficient dispatch plan in moments.
